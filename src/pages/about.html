About Mark My Words

Revolutionising writing feedback in education, one student at a time.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Dr. Emily Chen, a former Head of English with over 15 years of teaching experience, Mark My Words was born out of a passion for improving student writing and a frustration with traditional feedback methods.

Dr. Chen recognized that while providing quality feedback is crucial for student growth, it often came at the cost of overwhelming workloads for teachers. This realization sparked the idea for an AI-powered writing assessment tool that could enhance the feedback process without sacrificing the personal touch that makes great teaching so impactful.

Our Mission

To empower educators with innovative tools that streamline the writing assessment process, allowing them to provide more meaningful, personalised feedback and ultimately foster a love for writing in students.
